# Atheist - Piece Of Time

I love Atheist, it's all frenetic but belies a really skilled group of musicians who put out some of what ends up being some of the catchiest tech death ever.  Imagine, then, my shame at having not listened to this end-to-end ever!

Yeah, it's great, not as polished as they would become later, but still awesome.  Definitely will come back to this!
