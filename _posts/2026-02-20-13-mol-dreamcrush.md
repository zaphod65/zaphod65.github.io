# MØL - DREAMCRUSH

Blackgaze that leans more on the major key side of things, leads to an almost transcendent feeling to the album.  Emotionally a lot when it breaks from the trance-like parts, so this one is a lot to listen to in one sitting.  It's good though, brings the kind of intensity I want from an album like this, even if that means I won't come back to it super often.
