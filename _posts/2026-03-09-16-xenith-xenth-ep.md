# Xenith - Xenith

This is an EP of a band I caught live very recently, and is so far the only non-demo, non-single release from them.  It's fast, it's heavy, it's slick as fuck. Live, they were a force to be reckoned with, and I hope they go on to big things because this EP does catch at least some of that energy.

Also, shreddy as fuck, the frontman has _chops_.

Super enjoyable, will be keeping an eye on them.
