# Death - Spiritual Healing

Holy crap, this is again such a step up from even Leprosy, this is Death.  This is at the forefront of death metal again, leading the way.  This very definitely bridges the gap between the early material and the absolute genius of Human.  The change though, in only 3 years, from where they started to the musical ideas and lyrical themes here... madness.

100% listen again.
