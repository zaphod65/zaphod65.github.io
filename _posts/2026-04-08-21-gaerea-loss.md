# Gaerea - Loss

Oh.  Oh my.  I was sold this as being black metal, but grim and frost-bitten this is not!  This is post-black, taking elements of black metal and fusing them with what has come since to deliver an album that is in part screams and blast beats, but also metalcore and ambient and electronic...

It's emotionally intense in the best way, pushing and pulling the sounds they're working with to create tension, release, catharsis, and more besides.

This is good.  This is _really_ good, and I will be going back to their other albums in due course as well.
