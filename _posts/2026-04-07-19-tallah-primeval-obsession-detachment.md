# Tallah - Primeval: Obsession // Detachment

Tallah are part of the so-called nu-core wave, bands who take inspiration from the nu-metal titans like Slipknot and Korn and fuse it with heavier sounds, harsher vocals, the whole bit.  So the genre at its best is laser-targeted at me exactly... and this is a good example of it done well.

This is heavy, snarling, angry and also feels like it hints at something deeper.  I think I need to listen again, and I will happily do so!  Very enjoyable.
